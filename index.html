introducing me <3

Hi, it's Fahriye, pronounced "Fah-ree-yeh", & I'm a recording artist currently based in Boston, MA. I'm a vocalist, songwriter, and producer who creates pop music influenced by electropop, dance-pop, R&B, Turkish pop, and Turkish traditional music. My most prominent musical influences are Beyoncé, Ariana Grande, Taylor Swift, Hadise, Gülşen, Tarkan, Britney Spears, Edis, and Ibrahim Tatlıses. I'm a citizen of the world, at the intersection of three cultures (Turkish, German, American) and my music is the bridge that connects them all together.

My latest song "GENESIS" is out on all platforms now. It's a song that blends traditional Turkish instruments, such as the zurna, bağlama, and the darbuka, together with synths, bass, to give it a traditional, yet modern feel. It's a powerful anthemn about being young, ambitious, and resilient while chasing your dreams, despite how unrealistic it may seem to others.
